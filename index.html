Mental Stimulation: Hiking Vs. Software Engineer

I like to hike during my downtime. To me hiking has so many benefits. Walking/jogging through nature reminds me just how beautiful the world is; it's calming and peaceful. It's also challenging and overcoming any type of challenges comes with reward. To me reaching the peak is my reward especially depending on the type of views I get at the end of the trail. With programming language, with the exception of how taxing it can be on the body, this field would provide me with the challenges I need/want to stimulate my mind and give me the end reward satisfaction I'm looking for. As we age our body gets weaker so our physical capability drastically reduces but we don't have to let that happen with our mind. Of course, I understand sometime it's inevitable, but I want to be able to say at least I tried.


Work/Life Balance

The American dream consisted of buying house and having a family. More than that, what's important to me, is to spend time with my family. So this career would not give me the opportunity to do something I enjoy but also give me the time I'm looking for in order to spend it with my family. Most job just don't pay enough to have that balance even when there's 2 source of income. I'll do whatever it takes to get to where I need to be in order to have the life I want for me and my family.
